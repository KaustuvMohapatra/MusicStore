Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping options?

We offer standard shipping (5-7 business days), expedited shipping (2-3 business days), and next-day delivery options. Free standard shipping is available on all orders over $100.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on most products. Items must be returned in their original condition and packaging. Some restrictions apply to customized items.

Do you offer instrument repairs?

Yes, our in-store repair shop can handle most instrument repairs and maintenance. We offer services for string, wind, and percussion instruments. Contact us for a quote.

Do you offer music lessons?

Yes, we offer private and group lessons for various instruments. Our instructors are professional musicians with years of teaching experience. Visit our Lessons page for more information.
